Why Donate?

Gotham Tech Gargoyles is a student-led FTC Robotics team committed to fostering innovation, creativity, and collaboration. Your support helps us:

  • Build competitive robots – High-quality components, tools, and technology
  • Travel to competitions – Regional and national tournaments across NYC and beyond
  • Expand our reach – Outreach programs and mentorship for younger students
  • Develop future leaders – STEM education that transforms lives and careers
