Computer-Aided Design

Our CAD team creates detailed digital prototypes for every robot project. We use precision modeling, rapid iteration, and real-world assembly checks to turn ideas into parts that fit, move, and perform as expected.

Software & Workflow

We design with industry-standard CAD tools, then review every model with the build and programming teams. That collaborative process helps us catch clearance issues, improve strength, and refine mechanisms before fabrication begins.

  • 3D modeling of chassis, mechanisms, and assemblies
  • Linkage and motion studies
  • Bill of materials planning and cut list preparation

From Concept to Robot

CAD is where we move from sketches to a complete digital robot. We validate designs with virtual fits, then export files for CNC, laser cutting, and 3D printing so the build team can turn the model into a working machine.
